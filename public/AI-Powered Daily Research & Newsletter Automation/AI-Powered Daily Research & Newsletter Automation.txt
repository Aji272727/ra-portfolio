AI-Powered Daily Research & Newsletter Automation
Overview

This workflow automatically researches topics, gathers real-time information from multiple sources, generates AI-powered summaries, delivers them via email, and stores activity logs for reporting and analysis.

The system acts as an automated research assistant that works 24/7 without manual intervention.

The Problem

Businesses and professionals spend valuable time:

Searching for industry updates
Checking weather conditions
Looking up market trends
Researching current events
Writing summary reports
Sending recurring email updates
Maintaining reporting records

These repetitive tasks can consume hours every week.

The Solution

The workflow automates the entire information gathering and reporting process using AI.

Workflow Process
1. Scheduled Trigger

The workflow starts automatically at a predefined schedule.

Examples:

Daily briefing
Morning report
Weekly summary
Executive update
2. AI Research Agent

A Gemini-powered AI Agent receives instructions and determines what information is needed.

The agent can use multiple tools:

Google Search (SerpAPI)

Retrieves:

News
Industry trends
Market updates
Current information
Wikipedia

Provides:

Background knowledge
Definitions
Historical context
OpenWeatherMap

Retrieves:

Current weather
Forecast information
Location-specific conditions
Memory

Maintains previous interactions and contextual information.

3. Content Generation

The AI combines information from all sources and creates a structured report.

Example:

Today's AI Industry Update

• OpenAI released...
• Google announced...
• Weather in Manila...
4. Markdown Formatting

The report is converted into professional HTML formatting suitable for email delivery.

5. Email Optimization

A second AI model reviews and enhances the email content for readability and presentation.

6. Email Distribution

The completed report is automatically delivered through Gmail.

Recipients receive a professional, ready-to-read briefing.

7. Activity Logging

Important information is saved to Google Sheets:

Date
Topic
Summary
Delivery status
Generated content
8. Record Confirmation

A final AI process verifies completion and prepares data for future tracking.
